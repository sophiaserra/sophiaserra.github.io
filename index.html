"The National Economic and Development Authority Food Threshold and Dietary Requirement of Individual in the Selected Areas of Caloocan City"

Our primary objective is to investigate the adequacy of the P64 per person per day food threshold in the selected areas of Caloocan City, by evaluating whether it meets the nutritional needs of individuals based on factors like their monthly income and food expenditure per day. This involves analyzing the dietary requirements of people in selected areas, assessing the relationship between the food budget and actual dietary intake, and identifying any gaps between the two. We aim to highlight discrepancies and propose potential policy changes or interventions to ensure that the food threshold aligns with the true dietary needs of the community.
