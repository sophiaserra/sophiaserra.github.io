Mission & Vision

Mission

Our mission is to conduct a comprehensive analysis of the food threshold and dietary requirements of individuals in selected areas of Caloocan City. By understanding the adequacy of the current food budget, we aim to provide valuable insights that can inform policies and interventions to improve the nutritional standards and overall health of the community. Through thorough research and data-driven analysis, we strive to highlight the gaps and opportunities in food security and nutrition, and to advocate for measures that ensure everyone in Caloocan City has access to a healthy and adequate diet.

Vision

Our vision is to create a future where every individual in Caloocan City enjoys access to sufficient, safe, and nutritious food that meets their dietary needs. We envision a community where the food threshold is not just a benchmark but a reality that aligns with the true dietary requirements of its residents. By fostering a deeper understanding of the relationship between food thresholds and dietary needs, we aim to contribute to the development of effective policies and programs that promote food security and improve the quality of life for all residents. Our ultimate goal is to see a healthier, more nourished Caloocan City where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.
